Refund Policy

App: MyFinance — Personal Finance Tracker
Applies to: MyFinance Pro subscription (₹99/year) and any other paid features
Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Summary

All sales are final. No refunds will be issued once a payment has been completed.
Please review the Pro feature list carefully and use the free tier first to ensure the app meets your needs before subscribing.

2. What You Are Paying For

The MyFinance Pro subscription (₹99 per year) provides:

  • Access to the AI Finance Analyst with a managed API key (no need to bring your own Anthropic / Groq / Google key)
  • Access to the Pro Calculators suite
  • Any future Pro-only features added during the duration of your active subscription

Core features — transactions, investments, insurance, subscriptions, vault, SplitIt, dashboard, market data, and AI Finance Analyst with a bring-your-own API key — remain free, forever, and do not require a subscription.

3. No-Refund Policy

Because the Pro subscription unlocks digital features that are activated and consumable immediately upon payment, we do not offer refunds, partial refunds, or pro-rated refunds under any circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Change of mind after subscribing
  • Forgetting to cancel before auto-renewal (note: this app currently does not auto-renew — each ₹99 charge is a one-time annual payment, see below)
  • Inability or unwillingness to use a feature
  • Removal of an individual Pro feature in a future app version (you will continue to have access to the remaining Pro features for the duration of your active subscription)
  • Switching devices, browsers, or Google accounts

4. Renewal

This subscription does NOT auto-renew.
Each ₹99 payment grants you 365 days of Pro access. After expiry, the app gracefully downgrades to the free tier and you may choose to renew manually if you wish.

5. Failed Payments & Duplicate Charges

If you were charged but did not receive Pro access (e.g., the app shows you as a free user despite a successful payment), or if you believe you were charged twice for the same subscription period, please email pcbzmani@gmail.com with:

  • Your Razorpay payment ID (visible in the SMS / email receipt from Razorpay)
  • The Google account email you signed in with
  • The date and amount of the charge

Verified failed-delivery cases will be resolved by activating Pro for the correct account; verified duplicate charges will be refunded directly via the original payment method (this is a payment-failure correction, not a change-of-mind refund).

6. Feature Requests & Missing Features

If a feature you wanted is not present, the answer is not a refund — but we genuinely want to hear from you. We add features based on what real users ask for.

Please email pcbzmani@gmail.com with:

  • The feature you'd like to see
  • The problem you're trying to solve (this is more useful than the proposed solution)
  • Whether you're a Pro subscriber (Pro requests are prioritised but not exclusive)

You can also open a feature request on our public tracker at github.com/pcbzmani/myfinancedata/issues.

7. Bugs Affecting Pro Functionality

If a bug is preventing you from using a Pro feature you have paid for, this is treated as a service failure, not a change-of-mind refund. Email pcbzmani@gmail.com with the steps to reproduce, and we will either fix the bug or extend your subscription as compensation, at our discretion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The version in effect at the moment you make a payment is the version that applies to that payment. Subsequent changes do not retroactively affect past purchases.
