Privacy Policy

App: MyFinance — Personal Finance Tracker
Includes: SplitIt (group expense tracker), AI Finance Analyst & Pro Calculators
Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Overview

MyFinance is a personal finance app that helps you track transactions, investments, insurance, subscriptions, and group expenses. This policy explains exactly what data is processed, where it lives, and what we never do with it.

Short version: Your financial data stays in your own Google account and your own device. We have zero access to it. The only personal data we ever process is your email address — and only if you choose to subscribe to Pro features.

2. Data Storage

MyFinance (transactions, investments, insurance, subscriptions)

All financial records are stored exclusively in your own Google Sheets spreadsheet via a Google Apps Script deployment that runs under your Google account. The app connects to it using a URL you configure. The developer has no access to your spreadsheet at any time.

SplitIt — Group Expenses

SplitIt group data is stored in two places, both owned by you:

  • Your browser's localStorage (key: splitit_v3) for offline access
  • A separate Google Sheet you create and own — not the same sheet as MyFinance

Your identity within each group (your name) is stored per group inside splitit_v3 in localStorage only — it never leaves your device.

When you add a group expense and you are the payer, a copy of that expense (amount, category, description, date) is automatically added to your MyFinance transactions. This transfer happens entirely within your own browser — no server is involved.

AI Finance Analyst

The AI feature generates a financial health report by sending a summary of your data to an AI provider (Anthropic Claude, Groq Llama, or Google Gemini).

You can use the AI feature in one of two ways:

  • Bring-your-own API key: the key you supply is held in browser memory (React component state) only. It is never written to localStorage, cookies, a database, server logs, or anywhere else. It is cleared the moment you leave the AI Report page or close your browser tab.
  • Pro subscription: if you subscribe to Pro, requests are routed via our backend proxy using a server-side API key. We do not log the contents of your prompts or the AI response.
  • When you generate a report, a financial summary (income/expense totals, category breakdowns, investment values, insurance count) is sent from your browser to this app's backend, which relays it to the AI provider's API. The backend does not log, store, or cache this data.
  • Downloaded reports are saved only to your device — they are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Your use of the AI feature is also subject to the privacy policy of the AI provider in use: Anthropic Privacy Policy, Groq Privacy Policy, or Google AI Privacy Policy.

Pro Subscription & Payments New

Pro features (AI Finance Analyst with managed API key, Pro Calculators) are an optional ₹99/year subscription. The payment and identity flow involves the following:

  • Google Sign-In: when you choose to subscribe, you sign in with Google. We receive only your email address and display name from Google's standard OAuth response. We do not request access to your Google Drive, Contacts, Calendar, or any other Google data.
  • Razorpay payment processing: payment is collected by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant Indian payment processor. Card details, UPI IDs, and bank credentials are entered directly into Razorpay's checkout overlay and are never seen, transmitted, or stored by us. We receive only a payment ID and signature back from Razorpay for verification.
  • Email retention: your email is sent to our backend solely to bind the subscription to you and is stored alongside the payment ID and expiry date for the duration of your subscription. We do not use it for marketing.
  • Subscription token: after successful payment, the backend issues a signed token (HMAC-SHA256) which is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key myfinance_sub_token. This token contains your email, plan, and expiry timestamp. It is sent with each AI request only to verify your subscription is active. You can clear it at any time by removing the key from localStorage or signing out.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information beyond what is described in section 2 (i.e., email address only if you subscribe to Pro)
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • We do not share any data with third parties beyond what is strictly necessary for payment processing (Razorpay) and AI inference (your chosen AI provider)
  • We do not have access to your financial records, vault passwords, or group expense data
  • We do not retain bring-your-own AI API keys
  • We do not display ads

4. Browser localStorage

The app stores the following locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device except where explicitly noted:

  • myfinance_script_url — your MyFinance Google Apps Script URL
  • myfinance_sub_token — Pro subscription token (only if you subscribed); sent only to verify Pro status
  • Custom transaction categories you define
  • vault_pin_hash — your Vault PIN, hashed with SHA-256 — never transmitted in any form
  • vault_unlocked — Vault session timestamp (12h expiry)
  • Vault entries — stored in IndexedDB or your Google Sheet (your choice in Settings) — never transmitted to us
  • splitit_v3 — SplitIt group data including your per-group identity name
  • Dark mode preference and UI settings (sidebar_collapsed, etc.)

You can clear all of this at any time via your browser's "Clear site data" option, or by uninstalling the app.

5. Google Services

The app connects to Google Sheets and Google Apps Script using the URL you configure in Settings. This connection is directly between your device and your Google account. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles your data.

Security tip: Your Apps Script URL acts like a password for your sheet. Do not share it publicly. You can revoke it anytime via Apps Script → Deployments → Delete Deployment.

6. Market Data

Live market rates (stock prices, indices, currency rates) are fetched from public financial data sources (Yahoo Finance, Google Finance) via our backend proxy. No personal data is included in these requests.

7. Push Notifications

If you opt in to notifications (for insurance and subscription renewals), the browser-issued Web Push subscription endpoint is stored on our backend (Netlify Blobs) so we can send you reminder pings. The endpoint contains no personal information. You can revoke notifications anytime in your browser/OS settings, which also invalidates the stored endpoint.

8. Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data in transit and your subscription token:

  • HTTPS enforced on all pages (HSTS)
  • Security headers: X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy
  • Backend rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • CORS restricted to the app's own domain
  • Subscription tokens are HMAC-SHA256 signed; tampered tokens are rejected by the backend
  • Razorpay payment verification uses cryptographic signatures from Razorpay's webhook

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are under 13, please do not subscribe to Pro features.

10. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your local data anytime via your browser's "Clear site data" or by uninstalling the app
  • Delete your Google Sheets data by simply deleting the sheet from your Google Drive
  • Cancel your Pro subscription and request deletion of the email/payment-ID record we hold by emailing pcbzmani@gmail.com
  • Sign out of Google in the app to remove the cached email/name from session memory
