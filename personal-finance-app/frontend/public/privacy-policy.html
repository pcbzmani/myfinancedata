Privacy Policy

App name: MyFinance (includes SplitIt group expense tracker)
Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Overview

MyFinance is a standalone personal finance app that includes a group expense splitting tool (SplitIt). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is handled and how.

2. Data Storage

All your financial data (transactions, investments, insurance policies, subscriptions, vault passwords) is stored exclusively in your own Google Sheets spreadsheet via Google Apps Script. The developer has no access to your data at any time.

SplitIt group expense data is stored in your own separate Google Sheet (configured by you) and in your browser's localStorage. Group data shared with friends is routed directly through your own Apps Script deployment — the developer never sees it.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not have access to your financial records or group expense data

4. Local Storage

The app stores the following in your browser's local storage only:

  • Your Google Apps Script URL for MyFinance (to connect to your own spreadsheet)
  • Your custom transaction categories
  • Your vault PIN (hashed with SHA-256, never transmitted)
  • SplitIt group and expense data (splitit_v3 key) — stays on your device unless you connect a shared sheet

This data never leaves your device except to connect to your own Google account.

5. Google Services

The app connects to Google Sheets and Google Apps Script using a URL you provide. This connection is directly between your device and your own Google account. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles your data.

6. Market Data

Live market rates (stock prices, indices) are fetched from public financial data sources. No personal data is included in these requests.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.
