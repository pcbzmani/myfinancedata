Income Tax Portal
incometax.gov.in — Free downloads, login with PAN
🔗 Open Portal
Form 26AS — Tax Credit Statement
Shows ALL TDS deducted on your PAN — NRO interest, rent, capital gains. Most important document for NRI filing.
MandatoryFree Download
▼
  1. 1
    Go to incometax.gov.in → Click "Login"
  2. 2
    Enter your PAN number as User ID + password
  3. 3
    Dashboard → e-File → Income Tax Returns → View Form 26AS
  4. 4
    Redirected to TRACES portal — click Confirm
  5. 5
    Select AY 2026-27 → Format: PDF → Click Export
  6. 6
    Download and note all TDS amounts — they reduce your tax liability.
📌 What to look for: Part A (TDS on salary/rent/other), Part B (property sale), Part C (TCS). Each entry shows Deductor name, TAN, amount, TDS deducted.
🏛️ Open IT Portal Login
AIS — Annual Information Statement
Complete picture of all financial transactions — TDS, SFT (MF, bank deposits, property), interest, dividends, foreign remittances.
MandatoryFree Download
▼
  1. 1
    Login → Services → Annual Information Statement (AIS)
  2. 2
    Click the "AIS" tab (not TIS)
  3. 3
    Click Download icon → Select PDF
  4. 4
    Password is PAN in lowercase + DOB (e.g. abcde1234f01011990)
📌 Part B has your income breakdown: NRO interest, dividends, capital gains, foreign remittances. Cross-check each entry with your actual accounts.
🏛️ Open AIS on IT Portal
TIS — Taxpayer Information Summary
Auto-summarised AIS — categorised totals per income type. Use to pre-fill your ITR quickly.
MandatoryFree Download
▼
  1. 1
    Same path: Services → Annual Information Statement
  2. 2
    Click the "TIS" tab → Download as PDF
  3. 3
    On ITR portal → use "Pre-fill ITR" — auto-populates from TIS/AIS into your return
💡 Pro tip: "Pre-filled ITR" automatically pulls income data from AIS/TIS — eliminates manual entry errors for NRO interest, TDS, and dividends!
🏛️ Open IT Portal

Your Indian Bank (NRO/NRE)
Internet banking or branch — SBI / HDFC / Axis / ICICI etc.
Form 16A — TDS Certificate from Bank
TDS deducted on NRO FD/savings interest. Your Form 16 equivalent as an NRI — most important income document.
Mandatory (if NRO interest)
▼
  1. 1
    Login to your bank's internet banking
  2. 2
    Tax / TDS section → TDS Certificate / Form 16A → Choose FY 2025-26
  3. 3
    Download PDF — shows quarterly interest paid, TDS deducted (30%), TAN of bank
  4. 4
    If not online → email your bank's NRI desk requesting Form 16A for FY 2025-26
⚠️ Banks deduct TDS at 30% on NRO account interest. Your actual tax may be much lower — file ITR to claim the refund.
SBI HDFC Axis
NRO Account Statement (Full Year)
Complete bank statement Apr 2025 – Mar 2026. Shows interest credits, TDS deductions, all transactions.
Mandatory
▼
  1. 1
    Internet banking → Accounts → Statement → Download
  2. 2
    Date range: 01 April 2025 to 31 March 2026
  3. 3
    Note total interest credited and TDS deducted entries
📌 Extract: (1) Total interest on NRO savings + FDs, (2) TDS deducted (debit with "TDS" narration), (3) Foreign remittances received
Interest Certificate (FD/RD)
Annual certificate showing exact interest earned on your NRO Fixed and Recurring Deposits for FY 2025-26.
Mandatory (if NRO FDs exist)
▼
  1. 1
    Internet banking → Fixed Deposits → Interest Certificate or TDS Certificate
  2. 2
    Select FY 2025-26 → Download
  3. 3
    If not found → email NRI relationship manager: "Interest Certificate for all NRO FDs for FY 2025-26"
💡 The interest amount should match Form 26AS (Part A) and AIS. Contact your bank before filing if there's a mismatch.

Capital Gains Statement from Broker
Stock trades — buy price, sell price, holding period, STCG and LTCG. Required for Schedule CG in ITR-2.
Mandatory (if stocks sold)Capital Gains
▼

    Zerodha: kite.zerodha.com → Console → Portfolio → Tax P&L → FY 2025-26 → Download
  2. G

  3. A

  4. ✓

📌 LTCG exemption: First ₹1,25,000 of equity LTCG per year is tax-free. Broker shows gross gains — subtract ₹1,25,000 for actual taxable LTCG.

Dividend Income Statement
Dividends from Indian stocks and MFs are taxable under Schedule OS. TDS at 20% deducted by companies for NRIs.
Required if dividends received
▼
  1. 1
    Your broker's Capital Gains / Tax P&L report usually includes dividend details
  2. 2
    Check your AIS — lists all dividend income against your PAN
  3. 3
    TDS at 20% may have been deducted on dividends >₹5,000 — appears in Form 26AS
📌 Dividends are fully taxable at slab rates. Enter under Schedule OS in ITR-2.

Qatar — DTAA Documents
Required to claim India–Qatar Double Taxation relief
TRC — Tax Residency Certificate from Qatar
Issued by Qatar's General Tax Authority (GTA). Proves you are a tax resident of Qatar — required to claim DTAA benefit.
DTAA RequiredMandatory for DTAA
▼
  1. 1
    Go to Qatar General Tax Authority portal: gta.gov.qa
  2. 2
    Login with QID credentials or via Hukoomi
  3. 3
    Apply for "Tax Residency Certificate" — purpose: Relief from Foreign Tax / DTAA, country: India
  4. 4
    Download TRC — keep original + notarised translation if Arabic only
📌 TRC must contain: Your name, Qatar tax ID, period covered (FY 2025-26), confirmation of tax residency in Qatar per India-Qatar DTAA.
⚠️ If Qatar GTA doesn't issue TRC (Qatar has no personal income tax), submit an employer letter + Qatar ID as proof of residency. Check with a CA.
Qatar GTA Portal
Form 10F — Self-Declaration for DTAA
Filled on the India IT portal. Declares your tax residency in Qatar — paired with TRC to claim DTAA benefit.
DTAA RequiredFree — Fill Online
▼
  1. 1
    IT portal → e-File → Income Tax Forms → File Income Tax Forms → Search Form 10F
  2. 2
    Select AY 2026-27 → Fill: Country: Qatar, Tax ID: your Qatar ID, Period: 01-Apr-2025 to 31-Mar-2026
  3. 3
    Upload TRC as supporting document
  4. 4
    Submit + e-verify via Aadhaar OTP. Download acknowledgment.
💡 Fill Form 10F before filing your ITR. The acknowledgment number is referenced in Schedule TR of ITR-2.
Fill Form 10F on IT Portal

PAN Card + Aadhaar Linking Status
PAN must be linked to Aadhaar. Without this, PAN becomes inoperative — cannot file ITR or claim refunds.
Critical — Must verify
▼
  1. 1
    incometax.gov.in → Quick Links → "Link Aadhaar Status" → enter PAN + Aadhaar
  2. 2
    If NOT linked: Quick Links → "Link Aadhaar" → pay ₹1,000 fee + link
  3. 3
    If linked: all good. Keep your PAN handy for all forms.
⚠️ If PAN is inoperative, TDS is deducted at higher rates and you can't file ITR or receive refunds. Link immediately if not done.

Passport + Travel History (India Days Count)
Documents your days in India — confirms NRI status. Need count for FY 2025-26 and last 4 years.
Mandatory
▼
  1. 1
    Keep passport with all entry/exit stamps visible
  2. 2
    Count exact days in India during Apr 2025 – Mar 2026. Must be <182 for NRI status.
  3. 3
    Also count for last 4 years (FY 2021-22 to 2024-25) — relevant for secondary NRI test
📌 Qatar-based full-time employees with 0 or very few India days (<60) are clearly NRI. Document this for any future IT notice.

1
Pre-File Checklist (Before You Start)
✅ All 17 documents collected (check the checklist)
✅ Form 26AS downloaded — match TDS with your documents
✅ AIS downloaded — verify all income entries are correct
✅ PAN–Aadhaar linked and active
✅ NRO bank account pre-validated on IT portal
✅ Form 10F filed on IT portal (for DTAA)
✅ TRC obtained from Qatar GTA
2
Login & Start ITR
Go to incometax.gov.in → Login with PAN + password
Dashboard → e-File → Income Tax Returns → File Income Tax Return
Select: AY 2026–27 → Mode: Online → Start New Filing
Status: Individual → Form: ITR-2 → Click Get Started
🚀 Open IT Portal
3
Use Pre-Filled Data (Key NRI Tip!)
On the filing page → Click "Pre-fill ITR" button
Auto-pulls from AIS/TIS: NRO interest, TDS, dividends, capital gains
Review each pre-filled item and confirm or correct with your documents
Cross-check with Form 26AS figures — this eliminates most manual entry!
4
Fill Each Schedule
Schedule HP → Gross rent, municipal tax, home loan interest
Schedule CG → STCG/LTCG from broker/MF capital gains statement
Schedule OS → NRO interest, dividends (cross-check with AIS)
Schedule FSI → Any income from foreign sources
Schedule TR → DTAA relief — Form 10F details + Qatar TRC
Schedule TDS → Auto-filled from 26AS — verify all TDS entries
5
Tax Payment or Refund
Review Part B-TTI — total tax computed automatically
If tax is due: Pay via Challan 280 → Self Assessment Tax (300) → pay online via bank
If refund is due: Ensure NRO account pre-validated — refund in 6-8 weeks after processing
Enter Challan details in ITR before submitting
6
Submit & e-Verify (Critical!)
Click Preview and Submit → Review → Submit
e-Verify WITHIN 30 DAYS:
Option A: Aadhaar OTP — easiest, OTP on Aadhaar-linked mobile
Option B: Net Banking EVC — login to bank, generate EVC
Option C: Sign ITR-V physically and post to CPC Bengaluru

Download and save your ITR-V acknowledgment — this is proof of filing
🚀 File Now on IT Portal
7
After Filing
Track status: IT portal → e-File → ITR Status using acknowledgment number
Refund status: tin.tin.nsdl.com → Refund Status
Keep all documents for 7 years in case of scrutiny notice
Filing deadline: 31 July 2026 (non-audit individual)
